Contact Us

If you are experiencing a technical difficulty or need assistance, please reach out to us. For emergencies, we encourage you to contact a mental health hotline.

Mental Health Helplines

  • Emergency Mental Health: 1-800-273-8255
  • Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741
  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 1-800-273-TALK (1-800-273-8255)
  • Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA): 1-800-662-HELP (1-800-662-4357)
